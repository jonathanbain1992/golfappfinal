About Us

We are a popular golf club in the Cumbernauld area within the immediate vicinity of the stunning Palacerigg Country Park with access to a full sized, high quality, 18-hole golf course. There is of course, an active golf club resident on the course with a vibrant club house manned by a micture of passionate staff as well as volunteers. Be sure to pay a visit to our warm and friendly bar / bistro for some much needed refreshment after your time on the course as we're sure you'll need it!
